THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you create an account with demo, you give us your full name and email address, and create a username and password. If you want, you can give us a pseudonym instead of your legal name.

If you don’t feel like creating an account from scratch, you may be able to sign in through an account you already have with another service, such as Facebook.

Your user profile helps you customize your account and connect with other demo users. Your profile automatically includes your name and username. You can also add a profile picture and whatever else you’d like in the “User” field.
